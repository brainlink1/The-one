What is BrainLink?

BrainLink is not just a competition—it's a launchpad for innovation. We unite ambitious business strategists and technical engineering minds to co-create solutions that matter. Over several weeks, teams will analyze real challenges, prototype solutions, and pitch to industry leaders.
